Jose Rizal

The national hero of the Philippines.

A picture depicting Jose Rizal.

A few things Jose Rizal did/accomplished:

  • A Bachelors of Arts (Ateneo De Manila)
  • Licentiate in Medicine (Universidad Central de Madrid)
  • Polygrant Speaker in Ten Languages
  • Was a poet, essayist, diarist, correspondent, and novelist.
  • Author of two notable books, Noli me Tangere and El Filibusterismo

“One only dies once, and if one does not die well, a good opportunity is lost and will not present itself again.

-- José Protacio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda
